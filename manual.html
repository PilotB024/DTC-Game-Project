How To Play


Navigate to the play page and press start! Your goal is to survive as long as possible. Utilize your weapons and force abilities to survive as long as you can!


Controls

  • Use A and D to move left or right. You can also use the Left Arrow or Right Arrow.
  • Press W to jump. Press W twice to double-jump, draining some of your force meter. The Up Arrow key works the same way.
  • Use the Mouse to aim your blaster. Click the Left Mouse Button to fire your blaster.
  • Press Q to activate your shield. It consumes your force meter to block enemy attacks. Requires a minimum of 50 force to activate.
  • Press E to activate your lightsaber. Press SPACE to swing it.
