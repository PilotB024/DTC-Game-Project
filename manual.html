Controls

  • Use **A** and **D** to move left and right. You can also use the **Left Arrow** and **Right Arrow** keys for alternative movement controls.
  • Press **W** to jump. Press **W** twice to double-jump. The **Up Arrow** key works the same way.
  • Click the **Left Mouse Button** to fire your blaster and defeat enemies.
  • Press **Q** to activate your shield. It blocks enemy shots but has a cooldown, so use it strategically.
  • Press **Spacebar** to activate your lightsaber.
