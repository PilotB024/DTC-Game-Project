Controls

  • it is the A and D key to move to the sides, there is also a option to play with the left and right arrow key for more control options when moving

  • the W key is to jump upwards pressing it twice would alow you to do a double jump the same would happen when you press the up arrow key

  • pressing the left mouse button will allow you to shoot the bullets to defeat enemy's

  • when you press the Q key your able to bring up a sheild to block you from shots from the enemy's, but you have to use it when needed because you can't have it up all the time because of it having a cooldown

  • pressing the space key will allow you to bring up your lightsaber
